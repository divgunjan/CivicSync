Platform Features

Everything you need to
hold authorities accountable

From pinning a pothole to getting it fixed — every step is automated, tracked, and transparent.

Real-time Location Pinning
Click to pin or auto-detect your location. Google Maps + Nominatim fallback ensures accuracy across all Indian cities.
Auto Complaint Emails
Structured complaint emails are generated and sent to the relevant municipal body automatically — with Complaint ID, photos, and location.
Impact Score System
Every issue gets a live score (0–100) based on upvotes, confirmations, and days unresolved. Critical issues get fast-tracked for escalation.
Complaint PDF Download
Download a formal A4 complaint PDF with your photos, Impact Score, and authority details — for manual submission or personal records.
Escalation Engine
Issues unresolved after 7 days get followed up automatically. After 21 days, they escalate to higher authorities — with Impact Score in the subject line.
Analytics & Insights
Live dashboard showing most reported issue types, cities with most open complaints, average resolution time, and policy-level trends.
How It Works

Four steps to accountability

Report a civic issue in under 2 minutes. The system handles everything else.

1
Pin the Location
Tap the map or use auto-detect. Confirm your city and describe the issue.
2
Upload & Submit
Add up to 3 photos for visual proof. Hit submit — a Complaint ID is generated instantly.
3
Authority Notified
A formal complaint email is sent to the relevant municipal body within seconds.
4
Track & Escalate
Follow resolution status live. Unresolved issues auto-escalate weekly until fixed.
